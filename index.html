A Letter from the Owner

Welcome to Casa Agua Alta

There comes a moment in life when you realize how fortunate you've been. Casa Agua Alta was built during one of those moments — a time shaped by family, by polo, and by the extraordinary community that surrounds Careyes.

This home became the place where my children grew up connected to horses, to the discipline and beauty of the equestrian world. Over the years, this house held birthdays, long summers, quiet mornings, and sunsets that felt endless.

My hope is simple: that when you arrive you feel at home — and leave with memories of your own.

— Alberto Ardissone

Experiences in Careyes

Horseback riding on pristine, empty Pacific beaches at sunset. An unforgettable Careyes signature.

Boat Trip & Whale Watching

Private boat charters along the Costalegre. Whale watching in season (December–March). Snorkeling in hidden coves.

Paddleboard & Kayak

Turtle Sanctuary

Tennis & More

Courts available at Careyes Club. Jungle hikes, birdwatching, yoga, and visits to artisan villages in the Sierra Madre.

Costa Careyes Polo Club

World-Class Facilities on Mexico's Pacific Coast

The Fields

Two regulation Bermuda grass fields. Stables for 150 horses. 60 trained playing ponies. Dedicated grooms.

Match Days

Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday & Saturday. November through April.

Playing Rates

Practice chukkers from $150 USD. Tournament chukkers $200–250 USD. Lessons for all levels available.

Copa Agua Alta

Held annually during Easter Week. Co-founded by Alberto Ardissone & Giorgio Brignone. 160+ horses, players from 10+ countries.

Gastronomy

The Table at Agua Alta

In Careyes, the best meals happen at home.

Meals Made Here

Long, Unhurried, Deeply Personal

Breakfast always included — fresh fruit, eggs, bread, strong coffee. Private in-house dining on request. Seasonal, local ingredients. Menus customized for dietary needs. Cocktail hour on the terrace every evening.

The Dining List

Careyes' Finest Culinary Destinations

From iconic seaside institutions to hidden artistic gems — a curated collection that defines the sophisticated spirit of the coast.

Casa de Nada

A vibrant Mexican cantina offering creative small plates and antojitos paired with curated spirits. A place for conversation and discovering the playful side of local culture.

La Duna

Sophisticated open-air dining with fusion cuisine balancing bold international flavors and local textures. The ideal setting for a sunset dinner overlooking the ocean.

Lilo Beach Club

Modern coastal living at its finest. Fresh healthy bowls, premium seafood, and tropical mixology in a setting that defines the new luxury of the Mexican Pacific.

Playa Rosa Beach Club

The heartbeat of the coast. Refined Mediterranean menu with feet in the sand beneath iconic pink architecture. Fresh ceviches to grilled lobsters — timeless simplicity.

Malabar

Expert mixology and curated bites designed for those who appreciate the art of the cocktail and the beauty of a well-timed aperitivo in an upscale lounge.

Pueblo 25

A sanctuary for the culinary purist. Seasonal farm-to-table ingredients and daily Pacific catches. Mexican heritage reimagined with contemporary finesse.

Shio Sushi

Japanese precision meets Mexican ingredients. Clean, minimalist sushi emphasizing the absolute freshness of the local catch. Elegant and light.

Where You Are

A 35,000-Acre Reserve Where Jungle Meets the Pacific

Nine miles of untouched coastline. A polo club. A turtle sanctuary. An international community of artists, equestrians, and people who found this place and never fully left.

Founded by Gian Franco Brignone in 1968, Careyes has become a sanctuary of colorful casitas, cosmic art installations, and pristine beaches — one of the world's best-kept secrets.
