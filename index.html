A Letter from the Owner

Welcome to Casa Agua Alta

There comes a moment in life when you realize how fortunate you've been. Casa Agua Alta was built during one of those moments — a time shaped by family, by polo, and by the extraordinary community that surrounds Careyes.

This home became the place where my children grew up connected to horses, to the discipline and beauty of the equestrian world. Polo was not simply a sport for us — it was a way of life.

Over the years, this house held birthdays, long summers, quiet mornings, and sunsets that felt endless. It was never just architecture. It was where our life happened.

My hope is simple: that when you arrive you feel at home — and leave with memories of your own.

— Alberto Ardissone

Careyes Wonders

The Hidden World
of Careyes

Beyond the villa, Careyes holds a world of art, architecture, and natural wonder. These are the landmarks and experiences that make this coast unlike any other.

Copa del Sol
The iconic open-air amphitheater carved into the cliffside. Sound baths at sunset, live music, and cultural gatherings in one of Mexico's most dramatic natural settings.
Pyramideon
Gian Franco Brignone's cosmic pyramid — a spiritual site and architectural landmark for meditation and reflection.
Turtle Sanctuary
During nesting season, participate in the release of baby sea turtles on protected beaches. A transformative experience for all ages.
Cosmic Art
Careyes' coastline is dotted with cosmic art installations, colorful casitas by world-renowned architects, and private collections that turn nature into a gallery.
Cuixmala Reserve
The neighboring ecological reserve, home to jaguars, crocodiles, and over 270 species of birds. Guided excursions available.
